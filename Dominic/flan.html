Creamy Caramel Flan

Crème caramel, flan, caramel dessert, or pudim is a custard dessert with a layer of clear caramel sauce, as opposed to crème brûlée which is custard with an added hard clear caramel layer on top.

A small slice of this impressively rich, creamy flan dessert goes a long way. What a delightful finish for a special meal or holiday celebration

Ingredients:

3/4 cup sugar.

1/4 cup water.

1 package cream cheese, softened.

5 large eggs.

1 can sweetened condensed milk.

1 can evaporated milk.

1 teaspoon vanilla extract.
